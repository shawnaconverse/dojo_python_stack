### RED BELT MEANS YOU ARE A FULL STACK DEVELOPER

### RED BELT MEANS YOU DESERVE TO GET A JOB

  - Create
  - Read
    - Read Many
    - Read One
  - Update
    - auto population of form fields
  - Delete

  - basic, non-empty fields
